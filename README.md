## 🎯 简介 About

王者之弈是一款基于国际象棋的策略游戏，每个棋子都拥有独特的特殊能力，为经典象棋带来全新的战术深度。

King's Gambit is a strategy game based on chess, where every piece has a unique special ability, adding new tactical depth to the classic game.

---

## ⚡ 棋子特性 Piece Abilities

### ♚ 孤王之怒 King's Rage
己方兵全灭后，国王觉醒！可以瞬移至任意空格，周围8格仍可吃子。

When all friendly pawns are lost, the King awakens! Teleport to any empty square. Adjacent squares can still capture.

### ♛ 摄政皇后 Regent Queen
国王可被直接吃掉。王死后皇后接替，保留皇后走法，不会被将杀，5回合后判负。

The King can be captured. The Queen takes over for 5 turns with queen movement. Cannot be checkmated. Lose if 5 turns expire or queen is captured.

### ♜ 铁壁战车 Fortress Rook
战车拥有两条命！首次被吃后在3×3范围内复活。

Rooks have 2 lives! First capture: respawn in the 3×3 area around the capture square.

### ♝ 主教号令 Bishop Command
主教移动后，10秒内可命令一个友方棋子沿斜线移动。

After moving, the Bishop can command one friendly piece to move diagonally within 10 seconds.

### ♞ 骑士突袭 Knight Assault
骑士吃子后可连续L形吃子，最多5次，可随时停止。

After capturing, the Knight can chain-capture up to 5 times. Can stop anytime.

### ♟ 跳棋兵 Checker Pawn
小兵可跳过前方棋子（不吃），可连跳。前方连续两子时可跳吃第二个。

Pawns can hop over pieces ahead (no capture). With 2 pieces ahead, hop over the first to capture the second.
